About

I’m currently working primarily as a researcher with the Median Group. The name of this website, Countless and Absurd, is a reference to Hecataeus, reflecting my belief that logography is as necessary in post-modernity as it was in 6th century BC Ionian Greece.

I have few mostly unorganized and incomplete projects on my Github. If you’re interested in being marketed to, check out my LinkedIn and CV.
